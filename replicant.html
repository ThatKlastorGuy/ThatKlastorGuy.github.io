Replicant

Made in Unity with C#

Only available on desktop browsers.


Controls:

  • Left/right arrows or A/D to move.


  • Press space bar to fire. Hold space to charge up a potent shot that will make Replicants vulnerable to normal fire.

  • Press the up arrow/W key for a Nuke that will make every Replicant on the screen vulnerable.


Game play:

  • Thirty levels of retro arcade action.


  • Destroy everything on screen to progress.
