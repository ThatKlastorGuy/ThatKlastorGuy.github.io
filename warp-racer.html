Warp Racer

Made in Unity with C#

Only available on desktop browsers.


Controls:

  • Left/right arrows or A/D to move.


  • Press space bar or left mouse click to go faster/warp/break through asteroids.



Game play:

  • Pick up rings to increase your multiplier and refill your warp bar.


  • Smash through asteroids while warping for bonus points and to increase your multiplier. At regular speeds asteroids will destroy your ship.


  • Avoid beams! They are certain death.
